Privacy Policy

Effective: February 22, 2026
Last updated: February 22, 2026
App: Overdose Partner
⚡ Summary

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Overdose Partner works entirely on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no ads — ever. Your privacy is fully protected.

01

Information We Collect

Overdose Partner does not collect any personal information from you. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other identifying information.

Zero data collection. All app functionality — step-by-step guides, timers, dose tracking — runs entirely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

The app does not require an account to use. Nothing you do in the app is sent to our servers or any third party.

02

How We Use Information

Because we collect no information, we use no information. There is no data processing, profiling, or analysis of any kind.

03

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data to share.

Overdose Partner contains no third-party advertising networks, no analytics SDKs, and no social media integrations that could track your usage.

04

Device Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions, used solely for in-app functionality:

Vibration — used to alert you when observation timers expire during an emergency response. No data is collected or transmitted.

No other permissions are requested. The app does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device features.

05

Offline Use

Overdose Partner is designed to work fully offline. Once installed, the app does not require an internet connection. This is intentional — in an emergency, you should not be dependent on connectivity.

06

Children's Privacy

Overdose Partner is a public health tool intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children's personal information.

07

Medical Disclaimer

Overdose Partner is an educational reference tool only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always call 911 in an emergency. The information provided in this app does not replace professional medical training or advice.

08

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the new version at this same URL with an updated effective date. Given that we collect no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
